Request Direct Debit mandate

1. Request the Direct Debit mandate

By setting up a Direct Debit mandate you are requesting authorisation from your client to let you automatically take payments from their bank account.

2. Your client approves the mandate

After sending the request, your client will receive an email asking them to approve the Direct Debit mandate with GoCardless. This allows you to debit their account, up to a maximum of £5000 per month.

3. Take payments when invoicing

You can now select the automatic Direct Debit payment option when creating invoices or recurring invoices.

Direct Debit mandates

By setting up a Direct Debit mandate with GoCardless you are requesting authorisation from your client to let you automatically take payment from their bank account for invoices.

After sending this request, your client will receive an email asking them to approve the Direct Debit mandate through GoCardless. By approving this request, your client agrees to the maximum amount you can take and how often that amount renews.
